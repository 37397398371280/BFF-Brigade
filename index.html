Rules:

  • 1. Emergency Response Protocol for Holiday Events: In the event of a serious occurrence, holiday events will be promptly halted. Our top priority is to address urgent situations, including life-threatening conditions, severe distress, or adversity, with utmost care and attention, ensuring the safety and well-being of all involved.
  • 2. Respect: Treat fellow members with kindness and respect. Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or any form of harassment.
  • 3. Relevance: Keep discussions relevant to the group's purpose. Off-topic posts may be removed to maintain focus.
  • 4. No Spamming: Refrain from excessive posting, promotions, or spam. Quality over quantity is encouraged.
  • 5. Confidentiality: Respect the privacy of group discussions. Do not share private information outside the group without consent.
  • 6. No Hate Speech: Any form of discrimination, hate speech, or inflammatory remarks is strictly prohibited.
  • 7. Moderation: Follow instructions from group moderators. Report issues privately to maintain a positive atmosphere.
  • 8. No Self-Promotion: Avoid excessive self-promotion or solicitation. Seek permission from moderators for any promotional activities.
  • 9. Constructive Criticism: Provide feedback in a constructive manner. Negative criticism should be avoided without offering solutions.
  • 10. Language and Tone: Use appropriate language and maintain a friendly tone. Disagreements should be expressed respectfully.
  • 11. Compliance: Adhere to Facebook's community standards and guidelines. Violations may result in removal from the group.
  • 12. Inclusive Community Policy: We embrace diversity and welcome everyone, regardless of gender identity, sexual orientation, or personal preferences. Discrimination against individuals based on their identity or interests, such as being gay, furry, or transgender, is strictly prohibited. We encourage a supportive environment where all members, both existing and future, are treated with equality and respect.

What happens when you break each rule:

  • 1. Emergency Response Protocol for Holiday Events: Violators will receive a warning, but there is no immediate action. Repeat offenses may result in escalated consequences.
  • 2. Respect: Temporary removal from the group for the specified duration as a consequence for rule violation.
  • 3. Relevance: A 24-hour removal from the group due to inappropriate behavior.
  • 4. No Spamming: Warning for severe cases, with no immediate ban; allows for corrective actions.
  • 5. Confidentiality: Removal from the group for 5 days due to serious misconduct, emphasizing the severity of the violation.
  • 6. No Hate Speech: A 24-hour ban from the group as a consequence for rule violation.
  • 7. Moderation: Violators will receive a warning, but there is no immediate action. Repeat offenses may result in escalated consequences.
  • 8. No Self-Promotion: Temporary removal from the group for 1-3 days as a penalty for rule violation.
  • 9. Constructive Criticism: No ban or a maximum of 5 minutes removal for rule violation, allowing for flexibility based on the severity.
  • 10. Language and Tone: No ban unless deemed necessary; potential removal for 1 day to 1 week depending on the severity of the violation.
  • 11. Compliance: A 24-hour ban from the group as a consequence for rule violation.
  • 12. Inclusive Community Policy: Variable ban duration, up to 1 day, based on the severity of the violation.
